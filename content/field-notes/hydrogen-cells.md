**🔋 Hydrogen Fuel Cell & Electrolyzer Developers**

1. **Plug Power Inc. (NASDAQ: PLUG)**
    - Develops hydrogen fuel cell systems, electrolyzers for hydrogen production, storage, and fueling infrastructure.
    - Supplies fuel cells used in material handling (e.g., forklifts) and is expanding into mobility and power applications.
2. **FuelCell Energy, Inc. (NASDAQ: FCEL)**
    - Designs and manufactures fuel cell power plants and systems, including large-scale stationary power systems that can use hydrogen to generate electricity with low emissions.
3. **Cummins Inc. (NYSE: CMI)**
    - Through its **Accelera** brand, Cummins develops hydrogen fuel cell systems and electrolyzers for heavy transport and industrial applications.

### **🛠️ Industrial Gas & Hydrogen Production**

1. **Air Products and Chemicals, Inc. (NYSE: APD)**
    - A major supplier of hydrogen globally; produces hydrogen for industrial use and transport, including fueling stations and hydrogen mobility projects.

### **⚡ Hybrid & Supporting Technology Companies**

1. **Hyliion Holdings Corp. (NYSE American: HYLN)**
    - Focuses on power generation solutions that include hydrogen-capable systems (e.g., hybrid powerplants that can use hydrogen or other fuels).

### **🧠 Notes on Other Related Companies**

- **Ballard Power Systems** is a major _fuel cell developer_, but it is headquartered in Canada; its stock trades primarily on the Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX) and U.S. OTC markets rather than major U.S. exchanges.
- Some companies like **United Hydrogen Group** (planned Nasdaq listing) have been working toward public trading but may not yet be fully listed and widely traded.
