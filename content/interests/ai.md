A practitioner&rsquo;s interest in AI, separate from the noise. Most useful as a quiet companion: drafting, summarising, retrieving things from a long-running personal archive that would otherwise be lost.

The systems I keep are deliberately modest — local models where possible, plain markdown as the substrate, no dependence on a single vendor. The goal is something durable: a writing surface and a memory that will still be readable in twenty years.

Some of this work surfaces as projects on this site. Most of it stays in the background, like a well-arranged kitchen.
