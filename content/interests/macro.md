---
title: Macro Markets
caption: Capital as study
---

A personal study of how capital moves — across rates, currencies, commodities, and the businesses caught between them. Not as a profession, but as a long-form way of paying attention to the world.

The practice is mostly reading: central bank minutes, sell-side notes treated as primary sources rather than gospel, a small circle of independent writers. Positions, when taken, are sized for sleep, not for fireworks.

The aim is not to be right more often. It is to be _wrong less expensively_, and to keep a written record of the difference.
