## Premise

The available tools for retail macro research are either bloated, advertising-funded, or stripped of the qualitative texture that makes markets legible. Quiet Ledger began as a personal answer to that gap: a workspace built for someone who reads filings the way other people read literature.

## Approach

Three layers, intentionally separate. A structured market layer (prices, rates, releases) ingested daily from public sources. A note layer — markdown, free-form, with the same weight as the numerical surface. And a stitching layer that lets a chart cite a note, and a note pin a chart, without dragging either into the other&rsquo;s context.

> The hardest part was resisting the urge to make it _smart_. It needed to be quiet.

## Outcome

A workspace I open every morning. Used to draft commentary, archive trades, and keep a record of conviction over time — separate from the noise of feeds. Now being shaped, slowly, into something that can be shared.
