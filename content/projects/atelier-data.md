## Brief

A founder making beautiful clothes was being slowly buried by the back-of-house. Three spreadsheets and a notes app stood between cutting decisions and the next season&rsquo;s budget.

## Approach

No new tools introduced where the existing ones could be made honest. A normalised inventory model in Airtable, a clean handshake with Stripe, and a small set of weekly views that answered the founder&rsquo;s actual questions — _what sold, what didn&rsquo;t, what to cut next, what to wait on_.

## Outcome

Two seasons in, the planning conversation is now thirty minutes instead of a week. The clothes have not changed; the room around them is quieter.
