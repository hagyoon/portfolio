## Premise

Most days, I want the same five questions answered before I open any application. A briefing should be a closed object — read once, then released.

## Approach

A small set of jobs. One pulls overnight market moves. One reads the calendar. One filters the inbox to a handful of items that actually deserve attention. A model stitches it into prose, in a tone I&rsquo;ve trained over months. Delivered, quietly, by email at 06:45.

## Outcome

A small ritual that has outlasted three jobs and one move. The interesting lesson: AI is at its best when given a narrow job and a long memory of the person it serves.
