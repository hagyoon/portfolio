## Premise

Most people don't know what watch they want — they know it when they see it. Existing platforms are built for search, not discovery. Watch-Roll is built for the moment before you know what you're looking for.

## Approach

Card swipe interface to rate watches. "This or That" head-to-head comparisons. Taste profile builds incrementally from implicit signals — no explicit rating required. On the dealer side, aggregated preference data becomes market intelligence: which references are trending, what demographics are underserved, where demand is building before it surfaces in auction results.

## Outcome

A working prototype with a catalogue spanning mainline Swiss, independents, and microbrands. The B2B intelligence layer remains the longer-term conviction: there is no aggregated preference data in the watch market, and dealers price inventory almost entirely on instinct.
