## Premise

The watch internet rewards speed and spectacle. The interesting part of independent watchmaking lives further down — in the choice of finishing on a bridge nobody will see, in a maker&rsquo;s third or fourth movement before they got close to right.

## Approach

Slow notes. One maker at a time. Conversations recorded, transcribed, and edited like long-form journalism rather than reviews. Photography treated as a separate discipline, not an accessory. Nothing posted until it has sat for a week.

## Outcome

A library, now somewhere between thirty and forty entries. Useful to me; occasionally shared with collectors who write in. Designed eventually to live on a small, slow-publishing site of its own.
